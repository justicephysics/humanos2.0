## The Magic Trick of Modern Money

Most people believe money is created by governments, backed by gold, or earned through hard work. None of this is true.

In reality, money is created **from nothing** by central banks and commercial banks, in the form of **loans**.

- A central bank (the Federal Reserve, the European Central Bank, the Reserve Bank of India, the People's Bank of China) decides to create money.
- It does not print physical currency (though that happens too). It simply makes a **database entry** — a digital record that says a certain number of dollars, euros, or rupees now exist.
- This new money is credited to the accounts of **primary dealers** — a handful of large financial institutions that are closest to the central bank.
- Those dealers then lend the money to other banks, corporations, and governments — always at **interest**.
- The money circulates through the economy, but the **debt remains**. Every unit of currency in existence was originally borrowed into existence, and the interest on that debt must be extracted from the real economy — from workers, farmers, students, and consumers.

**The central bank has no funds of its own. It creates money from scratch. And it charges interest on the creation.**

Because all money is created as debt, the entire global economy rests on a foundation of borrowing. The numbers are staggering:

- **Global debt is over $300 trillion** — more than 350% of global GDP.

- **United States** — national debt over $34 trillion, roughly 120% of GDP.
- **China** — total debt (government, corporate, household) estimated at over 300% of GDP.

Every aspect of modern life depends on this debt:

- **Students** borrow to learn.
- **Homebuyers** borrow to live.
- **Entrepreneurs** borrow to build.
- **Nations** borrow to function.

And every loan, every bond, every credit card carries interest — a perpetual extraction from the real economy into the financial sector. The printing press is not a metaphor; it is a database. And it is running 24/7, silently transferring wealth from the many to the few.

## The Feudal Nature of the Central Bank Canal

This is the Canal Dogma in its purest financial form:

- **The narrow canal:** Money creation is restricted to a tiny number of institutions — central banks and their primary dealers. Only they have the power to create money from nothing.
- **The pressure gradient:** The new money flows first to the financial markets, inflating asset prices (stocks, real estate, bonds) that are owned overwhelmingly by the top 10%. By the time it reaches the real economy — wages, small business loans, social programs — its value has been diluted.
- **The heat:** Inflation. The silent tax that erodes the purchasing power of the bottom 90% while the top 10% watch their assets multiply.
- **The overflow:** When the debt becomes unsustainable, the system corrects — through financial crises, austerity, or currency collapse. The serfs pay the price. The lords are bailed out.

The central bank is not a neutral institution. It is the **chief financial engineer of the feudal order**. It creates the money, sets the interest rates, and decides who gets the first access to the newly created funds. The rest of society must borrow or earn what the lords have already received for free.
