## 1. The Vision — All Compute Moves to the Backend

Today, every application you run—Excel, Photoshop, a code editor, a video renderer, an AI assistant—requires local processing power. That power is trapped inside a chip in your device. That chip is manufactured by a feudal chain: ASML → TSMC → Apple/Qualcomm/NVIDIA. You pay for the chip. You pay again when it becomes "slow." You pay forever.

The Justice Physics Engine (JPE) inverts this entirely.

- **The Backend:** A shared, modular, photonic compute fabric. On Earth (cloud), then in orbit (Space Data Centre). It runs every application, every AI/AGI model, every spreadsheet, every game. It is cold, efficient, and near‑zero operating cost.
- **The Frontend:** A thin client—a screen, a hardware video decoder, an antenna, a battery. No local GPU. No local CPU race. Cost: ₹5,000–10,000. Lifespan: a decade.
- **The Connection:** The Hybrid Protocol (Image Lane + Cell Lane) transmits exactly what the screen needs, with maximum efficiency.

**Result:** Every app, every AI, every tool is now a cloud service. The handheld is a window into the entire digital universe. The feudal hardware chain collapses because local speed no longer matters.
