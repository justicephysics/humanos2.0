## 3. The Golden Rule

> **"Keep your Input Engine richer than your Output Engine. The more Input exceeds Output, the more excellent your life. The more Output exceeds Input, the more mediocre your life."**

— Mukesh Kumar, derived from Einstein's *"Try not to become a person of success, but rather a person of value."*

---

## 4. How to Use This Dashboard

1. **At the end of each day (or the next morning),** add one row to the Quick Status table. Estimate the rough percentage of time/energy spent on Input vs. Output. Be honest. No one else will see this unless you choose to share it.
2. **If you see an ⚠️ Unbalanced day**, ask yourself: *Did I produce output without enough deep input? Did I post, promote, or perform without first creating genuine value?* If yes, throttle back the Output Engine and feed the Input Engine.
3. **Every Sunday**, fill in the Weekly Reflection. Which engine dominated? What did you learn? What will you adjust for the coming week?
4. **Use this data to make conscious decisions.** The dashboard is not a performance review; it is a mirror. It shows you the truth of your rhythm, so you can adjust before the canal overflows.
